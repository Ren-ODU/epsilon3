Features

Intuitive Controls

Even with more features than previous models, our team has made sure to keep
the Epsilon III's controls and UI easy to understand. If further guidance is
needed, your new ship will come with both a manual and built-in video lessons
to introduce you to the many features available to you!

Efficient FTL

Building on the technology of the Epsilon II, the Epsilon III can travel
faster than light with far lower fuel consumption. Get where you need to be
fast and cover long distances with ease. The universe is yours to explore!

Chameleon Cloaking

Guarantee that your flying object remains unidentified no matter the
external conditions. Like earthly chameleons, this spacecraft can shift its
colors to match the surroundings, blending in seamlessly. From day to night,
clear skies to violent storms, no human will ever see you (or, at least, nobody
will ever believe them).

High-Power Tractor Beam

Ever struggle to abduct a stubborn specimen above a certain weight?
Say goodbye to such inconveniences for good! No matter how much they
struggle, nothing will escape your clutches ever again.
