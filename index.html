Features

Intuitive Controls

Even with more features than previous models, our team has made sure to keep the Epsilon III's controls and UI easy to understand. If further guidance is needed, your new ship will come with both a manual and built-in video lessons to introduce you to the many features available to you!

Efficient FTL

Building on the technology of the Epsilon II, the Epsilon III can travel faster than light with far lower fuel consumption. Get where you need to be fast and cover long distances with ease. The universe is yours to explore!

Chameleon Cloaking™

Guarantee that your flying object remains unidentified no matter the external conditions. Like earthly chameleons, this spacecraft can shift its colors to match the surroundings, blending in seamlessly. From day to night, clear skies to violent storms, no human will ever see you (or, at least, nobody will ever believe them).

High-Power Tractor Beam

Ever struggle to abduct a stubborn specimen above a certain weight? Say goodbye to such inconveniences for good! No matter how much they struggle, nothing will escape your clutches ever again.

Options

Standard Package

The Standard Package includes all afforementioned features along with a custom paint job of your choice.

Safety+ Package

The Safety+ Package includes the same features as the Standard Package with the addition of the following:
- Chameleon Cloaking+™ with stealth mode
- Tracking & ship recovery options
- Enhanced shields
- Helping Hand™ repair drones

Research Package

The Research Package includes the same features as the Standard Package with the addition of the following:
- Real-time translation system
- Large research suite
- Specimen containment & analysis units
- Long-range material scanner

Ultimate Package

The Ultimate Package combines the features of all other Packages for the best possible experience! In addition, this package includes a highly advanced AI copilot to help aid you on your journey!
